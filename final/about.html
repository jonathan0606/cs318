About NOTA

NOTA(None of the Above) is University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire's award winning, art, literature, and music publication. Published biannually the issues are entirely edited, and created by students and the content is chosen by the student body.

Biographical/Historical Note

NOTA is a literary publication by and for the students at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. Originally sponsored by the Theta Zeta chapter of Sigma Tau Delta and funded by the Student Senate, it is published twice annually. All students are invited to submit original work including poetry, fiction, essays, photographs, drawings, and other student works of art. Its predecessor publication, The Purveyor, is also included in the collection.


Content Decription

Copies of NOTA are available from 1973 to the present. Copies of NOTA's predecessor publication, The Purveyor, are available from 1969-1974. NOTA broadsheets (1988-1995) and administrative files relating to the cutting of NOTA's funding in the mid 1970s are also in the collection.
